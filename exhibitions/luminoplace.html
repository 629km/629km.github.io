Lumino Place

Interactive play equipment for childhood cancer patients — calming sensory feedback, gentle motion, and caregiver-friendly UX.

Overview

Lumino Place is an interactive play environment designed to stimulate curiosity and sensory development in pediatric patients. Through vibrant colors and dynamic light patterns, children are encouraged to move their bodies naturally, fostering learning and engaging in creative interaction. The central circular light platform responds to the children’s movements by changing colors and emitting sounds, creating a unique experience that combines play with sensory stimulation. The soft, organic shapes of the structure add warmth and familiarity to the space, while transparent windows gently blur the boundaries of the hospital's interior environment. This play environment goes beyond being merely a recreational area — it is a sensory-based immersive space that supports the emotional well-being of young patients and promotes social interaction among peers. Specifically designed for pediatric hospital settings, Lumino Place helps reduce anxiety associated with the clinical environment and serves as a developmental platform that supports children’s growth and comfort during their hospital stay.
