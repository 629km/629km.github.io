Lumino Dome

Immersive play environment for child patients — light, sound, and interaction designed to reduce anxiety and enable gentle engagement.

Overview

“Lumino Dome” is a healing interactive play space for child patients. Inside the dome structure, a main pillar and interactive pillars respond to children’s gestures and movements, generating diverse lights and sounds. These interactions go beyond simple visual experiences, inducing multisensory immersion, while projection mapping dynamically transforms the entire dome’s atmosphere in real time according to the content. Through this experience, child patients take the lead in shaping the flow of lights and patterns, becoming active agents in creating their environment. This offers them a healing play experience and emotional comfort within the unfamiliar hospital environment.
